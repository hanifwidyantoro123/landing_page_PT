about us

Our company, PT KKAMAN NAMU INDONESIA is located in Surabaya and the factory is located in Kapongan, Situbondo.
Our company produces wood charcoal made from 100% wood without any chemical mixture. Carbonization process takes around 5-8 days followes with cooling in drum around 8-12 hours.After that is cooling and weathering process is performed, which takes a minimum of 14 days before the final packing. Next, is cutting process and selection process into some size. Our wood charcoal is packaged using cardboard box and inner plastics. After that, our wood charcoal is stored in warehouse and covered with wooden mats to prevent moisture.

TYPES OF PRODUCT

  • ARANG PUTIH/BINCHOTAN (WHITE CHARCOAL)

    White charcoal (charcoal) is made from quality wood that has dense, hard density with combustion techniques for 5 to 7 days to produce white. This charcoal function is usually used for indoor BBQ fuel. They come in various sizes: L, M, S, S2, and S3.

  • ARANG HITAM/ARANG RIMBA

    This type of charcoal is usually used for outdoor bbq fuel and water filters. There's an OK Red size of 3 layers and OK Green 800g.
